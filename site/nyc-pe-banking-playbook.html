NYC Private Equity & Banking Opportunity Playbook - OBBB

Executive Summary

While the headline funding goes to defense/border security, there are significant opportunities for NYC finance professionals to capitalize on tax changes, QSBS expansion, and indirect plays on the major spending programs.

1. QSBS Expansion Play (Section 70431)

The Opportunity

  • 100% capital gains exclusion on qualifying small business stock
  • Increased limits: $50M → $75M gross assets
  • Per-issuer exclusion: $10M → $15M

NYC Action Items

  1. Create QSBS Advisory Practice
  2. Help startups structure to qualify
  3. Advisory fees: $50K-$100K per company

  4. Target: 20-30 NYC startups = $1-3M revenue

  5. Launch QSBS-Optimized Fund

  6. Seed fund focused on <$75M companies
  7. Market to HNW individuals seeking 100% cap gains exclusion

  8. Target: $50-100M fund

  9. Roll-up Play

  10. Identify companies approaching old $50M limit
  11. Structure acquisitions to stay under new $75M limit
  12. Exit before hitting threshold

2. State/Local Government Advisory

The Opportunity

  • $12.58B in retroactive reimbursements to border states (back to Jan 2021)

NYC Action Items

  2. Provide specialized OBBB reimbursement services

  5. Municipal Bond Play

  6. States will issue bonds against future reimbursements
  7. Underwriting opportunity for NYC banks
  8. Advisory fees on $10B+ issuance
